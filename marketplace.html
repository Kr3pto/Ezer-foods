How it Works

Supplier & Vendor

Ezer Foods’ distribution infrastructure is managed through a digital platform that enables real-time, endto-end data collection. The platform is also integrated with MTN mobile money and Vodafone Cash to provide cashless payments throughout the value chain. Here is how the service works:

  • 1. Ezer Foods staff register farmers on the app. Farmers only need a feature phone to receive harvest receipts.

    Farmers deliver their products to the nearest Ezer Foods collection centre.
  • 2. Ezer Foods sends farmers an SMS receipt and settlement is completed within 24 hours using mobile money.
  • 3. Ezer Foods manages the inventory through its warehouses and processing centres.
  • 4. Vendors place their orders on Ezer Foods’ USSD platform or through its call centre.
  • 5. Ezer Foods delivers products to vendors and records the details on the mobile app.
  • 6. Vendors pay for products using mobile money and Ezer Foods sends vendors an SMS receipt.
