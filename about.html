About Us

Mission

To bring collaboration and business intelligence together in one platform, which enables ignorant and passionate farmers to actively access financing and take part in cultivation using advanced tools and technologies available to plan, manage and produce quality crops to the maximum potential with ready market to potential vendors transparently.

Vision

Improved rural livelihoods and sustainable food systems in Ghana, Africa and the World.

Values

  • Respect and value for local knowledge and innovations
  • Value for the environment and ecosystems
  • Radical truth and Transparency
  • Professional, effective and informed by relevant science
  • Creative, flexible and innovative
  • Sensitive to gender
  • Quality, equality and ethics
  • Passion
